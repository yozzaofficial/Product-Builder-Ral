Italian Payroll Calculator (Milano)
Calculates the net salary for an employee in Milan based on gross annual income, including:

INPS contributions (employee portion)

IRPEF (progressive income tax) with work-related deductions

Regional and municipal tax

TFR (severance pay)

Supports monthly net calculation with 13, or 14 pays

Designed to estimate realistic take-home pay for different income levels while respecting Italian tax rules.
